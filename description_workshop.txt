Git for Terraria — a schematic/blueprint system with version history.

Select any region of your world, save it as a named schematic, and paste it anywhere else. Perfect for copying farms, wire contraptions, and intricate builds.

[h1]Features[/h1]
[list]
[*] Selection Wand: left-click two corners to select a region, right-click to clear
[*] Paste Wand: left-click to paste the selected schematic at any location
[*] Full commit history: save multiple versions of a schematic and restore any of them
[*] Captures tiles, walls, wires (all 4 colors), actuators, liquids, and slopes
[*] Schematics saved to disk — persist across worlds and sessions
[*] Press G to open the Schematic UI
[/list]

[h1]How to get the items[/h1]
Craft both wands at a Workbench using 10 Wood each.

[h1]Known limitations[/h1]
[list]
[*] Chest inventories are not saved (tile structure is, contents are not)
[*] Multi-tile furniture that straddles the selection edge may not paste correctly
[/list]
