Git for Terraria — copy, version, and paste your builds like a pro.

Select any region of your world, commit it as a named schematic with full version history, and paste it anywhere — farms, arenas, wiring contraptions, entire bases. Pasting costs real materials from your inventory by default, so it's a building tool, not a dupe machine.

[h1]Features[/h1]
[list]
[*] [b]Selection Wand[/b] — left-click two corners to select a region, right-click to clear
[*] [b]Paste Wand[/b] — live ghost preview of the exact tiles under your cursor, left-click to place
[*] [b]Version history[/b] — commit the same schematic as many times as you like and paste any past version, git-style
[*] [b]Material costs[/b] — pasting consumes the required blocks, walls, wires and actuators from your inventory; chat tells you what you're missing. Furniture is priced fairly (a table costs one table)
[*] [b]Templates tab[/b] — built-in schematics for everyone: Starter House, Arena Platforms, Hellevator Segment
[*] [b]Clean UI[/b] — draggable panel with schematic and commit lists, click-and-type text fields, delete confirmation. Press [b]G[/b] to open
[*] [b]Sharing-ready[/b] — upload/download schematics by share code once a sharing server is configured (Mod Config → Sharing)
[/list]

[h1]Getting started[/h1]
[olist]
[*] Craft both wands at a Workbench (2 Wood + 1 Iron or Lead Bar each)
[*] Select a build with the Selection Wand
[*] Press G, give it a name and a note, hit Commit
[*] Point the Paste Wand anywhere — the preview shows exactly what you'll get — and click
[/olist]

[h1]Configuration[/h1]
[list]
[*] [b]Paste requires materials[/b] (Gameplay, on by default) — turn off for free creative-style pasting. Server-side: in multiplayer the host's setting applies.
[*] [b]Schematic server URL[/b] (Sharing) — point at a schematic-sharing site to enable Upload/Download.
[/list]

[h1]Known limitations[/h1]
[list]
[*] Chest contents are not saved (the chest itself is)
[*] With materials on, tiles no item can place (trees, pots, natural cave walls) are skipped rather than pasted
[*] Multi-tile furniture straddling the selection edge may not paste correctly
[/list]
