Git for Terraria — copy, version, and paste your builds.

Select any region of your world, commit it as a named schematic with full version history, and paste it anywhere. Pasting costs real materials from your inventory by default, so it's a building tool, not a dupe machine.

[Features]
- Selection Wand: left-click two corners to select a region, right-click to clear
- Paste Wand: live ghost preview of the exact tiles, left-click to place
- Version history: commit repeatedly and paste any past version, git-style
- Material costs: pasting consumes the required blocks, walls, wires and actuators; chat lists anything you're missing
- Templates tab: built-in schematics (Starter House, Arena Platforms, Hellevator Segment)
- Clean draggable UI: press G to open
- Sharing-ready: upload/download schematics by code via a configurable server

[Getting started]
1. Craft both wands at a Workbench (2 Wood + 1 Iron or Lead Bar each)
2. Select a build with the Selection Wand
3. Press G, name it, hit Commit
4. Point the Paste Wand and click — the preview shows exactly what you'll get

[Configuration]
- Paste requires materials (on by default): turn off for free creative-style pasting. Host's setting applies in multiplayer.
- Schematic server URL: enables Upload/Download sharing.

[Known limitations]
- Chest contents are not saved (the chest itself is)
- With materials on, tiles no item can place (trees, pots, natural walls) are skipped
- Multi-tile furniture straddling the selection edge may not paste correctly
